**Urdu Poetry** is my creative soul. Beyond SEO and marketing, I write Urdu ghazals — a classical form of poetry built around themes of love, longing, loss, and beauty.

## The Craft

A ghazal is a poetic form with strict rules:
- Each couplet (sher) stands complete on its own
- The last couplet traditionally includes the poet's name (takhallus)
- Rhyme and refrain (radif and qafia) must be consistent throughout

Writing ghazals has sharpened my sensitivity to word choice, rhythm, and emotional precision — skills that directly improve my copywriting and content work.

## Published Work

My collection **Bedili** was published and is available online — a set of ghazals exploring themes of heartache, yearning, and the beauty of the Urdu language.

## Why It Matters

The discipline of poetry teaches you that every word must earn its place. That mindset translates directly into content that is tight, purposeful, and resonant — whether it's a 3000-word SEO article or a 30-word Instagram caption.

*"Har lafz ek dil ki baat hai."*
Every word is a story from the heart.
