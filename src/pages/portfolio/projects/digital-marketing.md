**Digital Marketing & SEO** — during my internship at **Cyber Infrastructure Pvt. Ltd.** (Sept 2024 – April 2025), I handled end-to-end digital marketing for multiple client websites.

## What I Did

- **On-Page SEO** — optimized title tags, meta descriptions, header structure, and internal links
- **Off-Page SEO** — backlink outreach, guest posting, link building campaigns
- **Content Writing** — case studies, service pages, and blog content
- **Intern Management** — mentored and reviewed work of junior content interns
- **Analytics & Reporting** — weekly performance reports using Google Analytics, Search Console, SEMrush, and Ahrefs

## Tools Used Daily

- Google Analytics 4 & Search Console
- SEMrush & Ahrefs (keyword tracking, backlink analysis)
- Google Sheets for reporting
- WordPress for content publishing

## Key Learnings

Developed a systematic approach to SEO — tracking rankings weekly, identifying drops, diagnosing causes, and implementing fixes. Learned to prioritize by impact and work across multiple client accounts simultaneously.
